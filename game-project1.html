2D Adventure Game

A handcrafted adventure experience by Miss D — blending storytelling and interactivity in a minimalist retro world. Step into a journey where every decision matters and every pixel tells a story. 🎮

Overview

This 2D Adventure Game is designed to capture the charm of retro pixel graphics while offering a narrative-driven experience. With intuitive controls, handcrafted levels, and branching storylines, players will immerse themselves in exploration, puzzles, and choices that shape the ending.

Key Features

🌍 Immersive World

Explore unique landscapes, from forests and caves to mystical ruins.

🧩 Puzzle Challenges

Solve logic-based and environmental puzzles to progress further.

🎭 Branching Story

Your decisions matter — multiple endings based on your choices.

🎶 Original Soundtrack

Atmospheric background music to enhance the adventure.
